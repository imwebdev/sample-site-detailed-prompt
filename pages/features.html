vMix Features

A comprehensive look at every capability that makes vMix the industry standard for software-based live production.

Camera Inputs

Connect HDMI, SDI, USB, and IP cameras directly. Support for multiple capture cards and devices simultaneously.

Pro Tip

NDI Sources

Discover and use any NDI source on your network. Send and receive video over standard Ethernet with ultra-low latency.

SRT & RIST Inputs

Receive remote feeds via SRT or RIST protocols for reliable, low-latency contribution from anywhere in the world.

Video Playback

Play back video files in virtually any format. Create playlists, loop content, and control playback speed in real-time.

Browser & Web Sources

Display live web pages, HTML5 animations, and browser-based graphics directly as inputs in your production.

Pro Tip

External Output

Send your program output to external monitors, capture cards, or NDI. Fullscreen output with independent preview and program feeds.

Recording

Record to MP4, AVI, or MPEG-2. Multi-format recording with independent quality settings for archive and editing purposes.

Multi-View Output

Configure custom multi-view layouts showing all your inputs on a single monitor for comprehensive production monitoring.

Title & Graphics Editor

Built-in GT Title Editor for creating lower thirds, name straps, tickers, and animated graphics with data-driven templates.

Pro Tip

Virtual Sets

Place presenters in photorealistic 3D virtual environments. Real-time chroma key with customisable camera positions and zoom levels.

Transitions & Effects

Over 30 transition effects including stinger, merge, wipe, and custom animated transitions between inputs.

Overlay Channels

Four independent overlay channels for layering graphics, tickers, logos, and PiP windows on top of your program output.

Audio Mixer

Full audio mixer with per-channel volume, mute, solo, delay, EQ, and compression. Support for ASIO audio interfaces.

Audio Bus Routing

Route audio through multiple buses — separate mixes for stream, headphones, and recording with independent level control.

Pro Tip

Audio Plugins (VST3)

Load VST3 audio plugins for professional processing — noise gates, de-essers, reverbs, and more on every channel.

Multi-Platform Streaming

Stream simultaneously to YouTube, Facebook, Twitch, and custom RTMP destinations with independent bitrate settings.

SRT Output Streaming

Deliver reliable, high-quality streams over SRT protocol for broadcast contribution feeds and point-to-point delivery.

Pro Tip

vMix Call

Built-in video calling — bring remote guests into your production with up to 8 callers in HD using just a web browser.

Web Controller

Control your production from any device with a web browser. Manage transitions, overlays, and audio from a phone or tablet.
